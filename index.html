About

Nasarawa Innovation Week (NIW) is a premier tech and innovation event designed to empower startups, entrepreneurs, and youth through cutting-edge technology, collaboration, and networking. This event brings together industry leaders, investors, and tech enthusiasts to shape the future of innovation in Nasarawa State.

Join us for a week of insightful panel discussions, hands-on workshops, hackathons, and networking opportunities. Experience the latest trends in AI, blockchain, fintech, and emerging technologies shaping the world today.
