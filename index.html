About

Nasarawa Innovation Week (NIW) is a 7-day celebration of innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship, uniting 10,000+ participants across the state and beyond. It is designed to accelerate inclusive innovation and economic transformation in Nasarawa through collaboration, creativity, and community.

NIW 2025 brings together innovators, entrepreneurs, policymakers, academia, investors, and development partners to co-create solutions for local and global challenges. From hackathons to exhibitions, it's a strategic move to position Nasarawa as a national innovation hub.
